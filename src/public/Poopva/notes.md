# Poopva 💩

**Strava, but for pooping** — log every movement, rank it on the seven-point Bristol scale, and share it with the feed.

Friends hand out **Poodos** (the only kudos that matters) and drop comments on your finest work, while your stats tab tracks streaks, weekly counts, and a wall of unlockable achievements.

Everything is multi-user and lives on the server, so the feed auto-refreshes every 30 seconds and the leaderboard your gut deserves never goes stale. 🚽
